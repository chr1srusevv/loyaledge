Create long-term, trusting relationships with our customers. What you'll do

  • Operate as the lead point of contact for all matters specific to your accounts.

  • Previous experience as an Account, Key or Junior Manager — or a comparable role.

  • Excellent listening, negotiation and presentation ability.
  • Genuine enthusiasm, energy and imagination.
